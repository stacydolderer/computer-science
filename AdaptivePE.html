Adaptive PE Lesson Plans

Exercises for APE

Warm up activities

  1. Jumping jacks
  2. Arm circles- forward and backward
  3. Reach for the sky
  4. Touch your toes
  5. Cherry Pickers
  6. Apple Pickers but don’t eat the apples
  7. Mountain Climbers

Stretching activities

  1. Sitting, one leg out and the other one bent. Reach for your toe. Switch.
  2. Sitting. Both legs out, touch your toes.
  3. Wall push-ups

Motor Skills

  1. High knees
  2. Butt kicks
  3. Step and slide, sideways

Daily Activities

  1. Just Dance Videos on YouTube or videos on Go Noodle
  2. Walking around the house or the block for 5 minutes
  3. Play Simon says
  4. Any activities that have the kids moving around.
